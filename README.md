# ✈️ FlightIQ
### Airline Performance & Network Intelligence Analysis

> Transforming airline operational data into strategic business decisions using **SQL, Excel, Power BI, and Business Analytics**.

---

## Project Overview

FlightIQ is an end-to-end Airline Business Analytics project designed to evaluate airline commercial performance, operational efficiency, pricing strategy, market demand, and network intelligence.

The project follows a consulting-style analytical approach, converting raw airline operational data into actionable business insights and executive recommendations. It demonstrates how data-driven decision-making can support airline management in optimizing revenue, improving operational performance, and planning future network expansion.

---

## Business Objectives

- Evaluate airline commercial performance.
- Analyze revenue generation and market demand.
- Understand airline pricing behaviour.
- Measure operational efficiency.
- Identify high-performing routes for capacity planning.
- Deliver strategic recommendations for airline management.
